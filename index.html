About

KEEPS.LOUDDER, launched in 2021, creates unique handbags that beautifully blend traditional Lao craftsmanship with contemporary design. We're passionate about eco-friendly production, gently weaving the timeless beauty of Lao traditions into every piece. More than just style, we're deeply committed to empowering local Lao artisans, providing fair income and preserving their incredible skills, ensuring that every handbag contributes directly to their livelihoods and the rich cultural heritage of Laos.

FAQ

How do I clean my bag?

Do not put this bag in a washing machine. It will ruin its shape and the faux leather. We suggest cleaning dirty spots by gently rubbing them with a slightly wet cloth. If you need to use water, use cold water to avoid shrinkage.


Should I be concerned about color staining?

Avoid direct contact with light-colored clothing to prevent the color from transferring. Since this is naturally dyed, a little color may shed due to sunlight, washing, or rubbing. With proper care, you can reduce fading and let the colors develop a unique, softer look.
